Wireframe Notes

Header

Simple, clear navigation. Logo on the left, primary navigation links on the right.

Hero

Above the fold. Must have a compelling headline and a single, clear call-to-action (CTA).

Highlights

Quickly scannable benefits of the course. Use icons in the final prototype.

Curriculum

A simple list of modules. In the prototype, these will be expandable to show more detail.

Testimonials

Social proof is crucial. A two-column grid works well on desktop.

Pricing

The final, unavoidable ask. Make it a single, clear offer with another strong CTA.
